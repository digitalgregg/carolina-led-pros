HealthSmart Pharmacy replaced 45 legacy 2*4 troffers with modern LED panels. The owner-installed upgrade immediately lowered the annual lighting bill from $1,917 to $416, saving $1,501 per year. With a 3-month payback and an estimated $18,661 in savings over 10 years, the project delivers bright, uniform light for customers and staff while keeping operating costs predictable.

1. Energy & Light Quality

New LED panels provide even, glare-controlled lighting that improves product visibility at the counter and in aisles. The pharmacy now enjoys consistent color and brightness throughout the space.

2. Operational Simplicity

Standardized 2*4 panels reduce maintenance complexity and downtime. Longer lifespans mean fewer interruptions and less time spent on relamping or troubleshooting.

3. Cost & Payback

The project's $1,501 annual savings drove a 3-month payback and a 600% ROI over two years, with 10-year savings estimated at $18,661-a high-impact result for a small business.
