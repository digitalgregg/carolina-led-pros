The Challenge

Legacy lighting fixtures across 1,936 locations created high operating costs averaging 3,432 hours annually, consuming 663,903 watts of energy. Kennedy Middle School needed a cost-effective, sustainable solution that improved lighting quality without disrupting educational activities.

Our Approach

1. Targeted Consultation & Plan

We documented run-times, fixture counts, mounting heights, and lighting needs. Modeled savings and payback to optimize investment across all school areas.

2. High-Efficiency LEDs

Installed energy-saving LED fixtures tailored to classroom, hallway, and facility requirements, reducing total wattage to 210,856 watts for even, comfortable light.

3. Clean, Phased Installation

Scheduled work around school schedules and daily operations. Included safe disposal of old fixtures and ballast equipment.

4. Rebate & Incentives

Secured Duke Smart Saver utility incentives to reduce upfront costs and accelerate ROI.

Results

Financial

  • Total Cost: $121,020

  • Duke Smart Saver Rebate: $75,739

  • Net Investment: $45,281

  • Payback: 11.5 months

  • Average Monthly Savings: $3,800

  • Yearly Energy & Maintenance Savings: $45,000

  • 10-Year Total Savings: $559,956

Operational

  • Improved light quality in classrooms and common areas with brighter, more uniform illumination.

  • Reduced maintenance calls with standardized, long-life LED fixtures.

  • Lower heat output improved building efficiency and reduced HVAC load.

Sustainability

  • 68% energy reduction achieved — from 663,903 watts to 210,856 watts.

  • 1,561,368 kWh saved annually — significantly reducing energy consumption and carbon emissions.

  • Supports Kennedy Middle School's long-term sustainability and environmental education goals.
