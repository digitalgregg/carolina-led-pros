About Charlotte LED Pros
After a 15 year career in NASCAR technology, Dave Kobuszewski pivoted when he saw the need to help the businesses throughout the Carolinas save energy by Going Green and converting to LED Lighting. The company focuses on delivering best-in-class customer service with outstanding installation and guaranteed energy-saving results.

Charlotte LED Pros electricians have more than 40 years experience led by Jason Sheridan. Our team works around busy schedules including weeknights and weekends which are the best time to avoid interfering with day-to-day business operations.

A single small business can recoup their out of pocket expenses in 12 months. A midsize to large business will show project payback in 30 months.

The products we specialize in offer extreme flexibility for customers. Both in design and functionality. Whether you're looking for trusted, top-tier U.S. brands like Lithonia or exploring innovative, cost-effective options from international manufacturers, we have the expertise and network to deliver it.

From design to budget, Charlotte LED Pros ensures you have the perfect lighting solution tailored to your needs.
